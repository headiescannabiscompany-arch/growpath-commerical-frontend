# Commercial IA (v1)

## Scope (locked)
Commercial = Pro Single surface + business overlays.
Commercial must not be a separate weird app; it reuses Single User IA and adds overlays where appropriate.

---

## IN (Functional targets)
- Commercial Inventory (list + detail)
- Commercial Feed/Alerts (read-only ok if endpoints exist)

---

## OUT (Planned in v1)
- Courses Creator / Publishing workflows
- Storefront (products, orders, links)
- Earnings / Payouts
- Compliance

---

## Inventory (v1)
Priority entity is Commercial Inventory.
- Must support: list + detail
- Update is required (v1)
- Create can be v1.1 (planned)

Commercial Inventory is separate scope from Facility Inventory.

---

## Done criteria (commercial)
- Commercial user lands in commercial shell
- Inventory list loads
- Inventory detail loads
- At least one update mutation works
- Feed/Alerts render read-only without broken actions
