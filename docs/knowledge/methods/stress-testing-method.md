# Stress Testing and Recovery

Create a stress/recovery record from intentional tests or automatically after missed watering, severe dryback, overwatering, heat/RH excursions, light stress, high EC, pH drift or diagnosis events.

Track stress type, stage, severity, duration, response, recovery time, lasting damage, new-growth recovery, intersex signs, quality impact and keeper impact. Do not invent severity/resilience scores from incomplete history.

Feed outcomes into Pheno Hunting, Genetics Inventory, Crop Steering, Run Comparison and AI retrieval. Recovery without lasting quality/stability loss is evidence of resilience; stalling, prolonged wilt, intersex expression or quality loss indicates excessive pressure.
