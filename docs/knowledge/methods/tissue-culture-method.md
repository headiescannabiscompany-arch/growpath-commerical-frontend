# Tissue Culture

Separate mother bank, production line, ordinary cold storage and validated cryopreservation. Track genetics/source lineage, batch/parent transfer, vessel/rack/shelf, media lot/recipe, sterilization run, SOP, technician custody, transfer cycles, environment telemetry, contamination disposition, pathogen/index testing, genetic stability, rooting/acclimation cohorts, storage entry/retrieval and recovery.

Counts, IDs, lots, temperatures and lab results must be recorded evidence. Media may support visible patterns but not identify microorganisms. Compare protocol survival, regrowth, contamination, labor and cost per surviving plant. Require traceability and quality-control tasks before transfer, storage or production release.
